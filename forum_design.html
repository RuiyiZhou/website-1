Project Description

The forum was designed to get timely feedbacks from users about the newly-released version of QQ Browser and offer a place where users from all over the world can communicate with each other. This forum was first released in December, 2013. Till June, 2014, it owned over 10000 members and nearly 100 thousand postings.
