QQ Browser Forum Design

An official forum supporting QQ Browser, serving as a discussion board for users, and a method to collect feedback from users.

Enter Forum

   

Team Role

    Product Manager, UX Designer

Duration

    10/2013 - 06/2014

Main Functions

  • A method to collect feedback
  • A discussion board for users
   

Project Description

The forum was designed to get timely feedback from users about the newly-released version of QQ Browser and serve as a discussion board for users. This forum was first released in December, 2013. Till June, 2014, it owned over 10000 members and nearly 100 thousand postings.
